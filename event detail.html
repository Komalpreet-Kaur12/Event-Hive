About HackSphere 2025

HackSphere is the annual flagship hackathon of NIT Amritsar — a 48-hour team-based event where students come together to build innovative solutions across domains like AI/ML, FinTech, HealthTech, and Sustainability.

Whether you're a first-time hacker or a seasoned builder, HackSphere offers an environment to learn, collaborate, and create. Participants will get access to mentors, workshops, and cloud credits from our sponsors.

📋 Rules & Eligibility
  • Open to all undergraduate students (B.Tech / BCA / BSc)
  • Teams must have 3–5 members; solo registrations not allowed
  • All team members must register individually on EventHive
  • Projects must be built during the hackathon window (no pre-built work)
  • Use of open-source libraries is allowed; plagiarism leads to disqualification
  • At least one team member must be present on-site on both days
  • Decisions of the judges are final and binding
